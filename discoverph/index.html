About Us

"Best place to find & explore everything that you need"

Discover PH is a top online magazine in the Philippines. We’re a team of writers, photographers, graphic artists, videographers, and storytellers who simply want to share our stories of adventures and discoveries.

We capture the best and undiscovered treasures of Philippines & beyond. Our mission to help support local craft and talent as well as showcase just what the Philippines is all about in terms of travel, culture and hospitality. Its vision is to create a community of Filipinos who are linked together through their love of food, travel, lifestyle and adventures.
